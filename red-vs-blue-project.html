RED vs BLUE

Project Details

Tools Used: Autodesk Maya (3D Modelling, Animation, Textures), CapCut (Video Editing)

Duration: 2 months (full-time)

Restrictions: My first animation experience, developed in a short time frame.

Description: RED vs BLUE is a football animation I created using Autodesk Maya focused on animation and movement. The project required me to animate a short scene that demonstrated character interaction, timing, and believable motion. I decided to animate a football match scenario between two crayon characters—one wearing red and the other blue. The scene features running, saving, diving, shooting and celebrating actions to bring the intensity of a real football moment to life through animation. This was my first experience working on a full animated sequence in Maya, which helped me learn essential animation skills like keyframing, timing, camera angles, and character posing. I also learned how to make the animation flow naturally to reflect the energy and rhythm of a football match. Through this project, I combined my passion for football with my growing interest in 3D animation, and it gave me the opportunity to tell a story entirely through movement.
